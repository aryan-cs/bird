## 1. Bore centerline clearance (the real print-readiness check)

For each new EDF bore, samples 20 points along the centerline at X=±28.0, Z=-5.0 and tests whether each point is INSIDE the modified mesh (= blocked by plastic) or OUTSIDE (= clear air). 0% blocked means you can slide an EDF straight through.

| Part   | Left bore blocked | Right bore blocked |

| fuse2 | 0% | 0% |
| fuse3 | 0% | 0% |
| fuse4 | 10% | 10% |

**Interpretation**: anything above ~5% indicates leftover plastic blocking the bore — you'd need to either clean it up in CAD or hot-knife it during assembly.
